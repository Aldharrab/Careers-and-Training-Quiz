Pen Tester

Future you

A penetration tester is a cybersecurity professional who simulates cyberattacks on systems, networks, or web applications to identify vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them. They use various hacking tools, techniques, and methodologies to evaluate security measures, often targeting weaknesses in firewalls, operating systems, and network configurations. Pen testers document their findings in detailed reports and suggest ways to fix the vulnerabilities. Their work is crucial for strengthening the security posture of organizations and ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations.
